Sembcorp Refines Faster, More Efficient Workflows with K2

Learn how Sembcorp Industries, a leading global energy, water and marine group, reduced the time it took to create workflows and e-forms by 96% with K2.

Sembcorp was in need of a robust, flexible workflow engine to effectively cater to their various processes. The solution also needed to be fully interoperable with their current Microsoft tools and development platform.

The Sembcorp IT department selected K2 and working closely with K2’s consultants, transformed the company’s entire workflow development and management system.

“Our IT team now has a dependable and robust workflow system, allowing us to develop workflows and forms with greater ease and speed today and in the future.” – Kuan Choon Cheit, Senior Manager, Group Information Technology, Sembcorp Industries
